About Me

I'm an experienced Digital Product/Web/UX/UI Designer, specializing in AI-driven design. With over 35 years in the field, I use AI to personalize user experiences, make interfaces more intuitive, and predict user needs. My skills include rapid prototyping and data analysis with AI, ensuring my designs are user-focused and efficient. I'm committed to creating accessible, engaging digital products that blend aesthetics with innovative AI technology.

1984

I began in digital graphics, pioneering in 2D and 3D animation and exploring digital art's potential.

1996

With the rise of the internet, I moved into web design, using Macromedia Flash for dynamic websites and focusing on visual aesthetics and user experience.

2005

I shifted to enhancing web interactivity, making online experiences more engaging and user-centric.

2011

I adapted to the mobile era, focusing on responsive design for consistent experiences across devices.

2022

Recently, I've been integrating AI with UX/UI design to personalize user experiences and improve efficiency, emphasizing the human aspect of design.

Extensive Design Experience

My digital design career features global collaborations, showcasing a portfolio rich in innovative techniques and designs that connect with and inspire audiences.
