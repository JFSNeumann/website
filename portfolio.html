Over an eight-year journey, I spearheaded the revamp of CGI's Contract Administration Tracking System (CATS), a pivotal tool for Project-Based Contract Administrators (PBCAs). The overhaul focused on elevating operational efficiency and meeting stringent compliance standards.

Project Scope

The project demanded an in-depth grasp of federal housing regulations and PBCA-specific needs. I implemented scalable workflows to optimize operations.

Overcoming Obstacles

Migration brought challenges like data integrity and system downtime. My design approach mitigated these issues, ensuring a smooth transition for end-users.

Innovative Solutions

Leveraging automation and AI, CATS reduces administrative burdens and frees up PHA staff for mission-critical activities.

Results and Impact

CATS has transformed into an indispensable tool for PBCAs, consistently maintaining regulatory compliance and enhancing operational efficiency.
